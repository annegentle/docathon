Docathon

What's a docathon?

It's kind of like a hackathon, but focused on developing material and tools for documentation. Hackers of all skill-levels are welcome. The docathon will be held the week of March 6th, 2017. We'll have a kickoff event on the 6th, and then a week of hacking and documenting. We hope you'll join us in person or online!

Why a docathon?

Everybody knows how important documentation is. However, as important as it is, we often don't give documentation enough attention. Docstrings are incomplete or missing argument explanations, examples don't compile or reveal outdated APIs, tutorials are often idiosynchratic (or not even there). The docathon is about changing that.

How do I join?

We welcome both individuals and projects looking to participate, either locall or remotely. If you want to host your own remote Docathon party, click here. If you want to sign up for the event, click one of the buttons below and answer a couple questions.
